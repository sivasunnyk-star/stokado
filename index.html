What is SIP?
Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) lets you invest a fixed amount every month in mutual funds. Even ₹500/month can build wealth!

Example: ₹5,000/month at 12% for 20 years = ₹49.9 Lakhs
💰
Rule of 72
Divide 72 by your return rate to know when your money doubles.

At 12%: 72÷12 = 6 years to double!
At 8%: 72÷8 = 9 years
At 6%: 72÷6 = 12 years

MF Types Explained
Large Cap — Top 100 companies. Stable, lower risk
Mid Cap — 101-250 companies. Higher growth, more risk
Small Cap — Below 250. Highest growth potential + risk
ELSS — Tax saving + equity returns
Index Fund — Tracks Nifty/Sensex, lowest cost
🏠
Buy House or Invest in MF?
House: 6-8% returns, illiquid, high cost
MF SIP: 12-15% historical returns, liquid, start from ₹500

Verdict: SIP first, house later — build your down payment via SIP!
